01h Set text-mode cursor shape
	CH = Scan Row Start, 
	CL = Scan Row End
		Normally a character cell has 8 scan lines, 0–7. 
		So, CX=0607h is a normal underline cursor, CX=0007h is a full-block cursor. 
		If bit 5 of CH is set, that often means "Hide cursor". So CX=2607h is an invisible cursor.
		Some video cards have 16 scan lines, 00h-0Fh.
		Some video cards don't use bit 5 of CH. With these, make Start>End (e.g. CX=0706h)
